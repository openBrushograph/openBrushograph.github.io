About this Tool:
Brushograph: Sequencer Platinum is a dedicated sonic and rhythmic exploration tool for the FluidNC-driven Brushograph. It combines a multi-axis step sequencer with persistent drone engines, allowing for complex mechatronic soundscapes and generative drawing patterns.

AI Development Context:
This version is a specialized fork of the Ultimate Platinum Studio, optimized for rhythmic experimentation and sonic performance. Developed autonomously in the Antigravity IDE using Gemini 3 Flash for rapid iteration, leveraging the established mechatronic communication patterns of the OpenBrushograph ecosystem.
